Truth #12

A small, obsessed niche is better than a large lukewarm market

Executive Summary

A founder once proudly declared his market was "everyone with a smartphone." An investor laughed. I made the same mistake, building a tool "for teams" that resonated with no one. We only found traction when we niched down to indie game developers and solved their specific, painful problems. Choosing a niche feels like limiting your potential, but it's actually focusing your power. 100 people who love you are infinitely more valuable than 10,000 who "kinda like" you. Win a small, passionate beachhead first. From there, you can expand your empire.
